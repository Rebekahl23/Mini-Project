Rehoboth Boating

The Rehoboth Restaurent offers top-notch boating facilities, providing a convenient and sameless experience for guests arriving by water. It features a well-maintained docking area with plenty of space to safely moor boats. The docks are easily accessible, making it simple for boaters to park and enjoy a meal without hassle. The restaurent's waterfront location ensures dines enjoy scenic views of the water, adding to the overall dining experience. Whether you're arriving dining for a casual meal or a special occasion, the boating facilities privide a unique andenjoyable way to access the Restaurent.
